Packaging Process

1.Packaging Material Selection

Choose food-grade containers: Glass bottles PET plastic bottles Sachets (industrial scale) Ensure containers are clean, odor-free, and undamaged.

2.Container Washing

Wash bottles with clean water and food-safe detergent Rinse thoroughly to remove soap residues.

3. Sterilization

Sterilize containers by: Boiling in hot water (10–15 minutes), or Steam sterilization Allow bottles to drain upside down.

4. Cooling & Inspection

Allow bottles to cool Inspect for: Cracks Deformation Foreign particles.

5. Hot Filling

Fill bottles while folere drink is still warm (60–70°C) Leave small headspace (about 1–2 cm).

6.Sealing / Capping

Seal immediately using: Bottle caps Screw tops Heat sealing (sachets) Ensure airtight closure.

7. Storage & Distribution

Store in: Refrigeration (short shelf life) Cool, dry place away from sunlight Transport carefully to avoid breakage.
