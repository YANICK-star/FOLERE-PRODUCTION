Production Process

1. Raw Material Selection

Select fresh, dry folere (hibiscus) calyces free from dirt and mold.

2. Sorting and Cleaning

Sort the folere to remove stones, dust, and damage calyes, wash thoroughly with clean water.

3. Extraction (Brewing)

Boil clean water Add washed folere into boiling water Allow to boil for 10–20 minutes Turn off heat and allow to steep for 30–60 minutes.

4. Filtration

Clean cloth Fine sieve Food-grade filter Remove all solid residues.

5. Pasteurization

Heat the drink to 65–75°C for 15–30 minutes Do not boil Allow to cool gradually.

6. Cooling

Allow drink to cool to room temperature Avoid open exposure (use covered containers)

7. Quality Control

Check: Taste Color Smell Discard if fermentation or gas buildup occurs.

8. Storage

Refrigerated: 7–14 days Room temperature (pasteurized + preservative): 1–3 months Avoid sunlight and heat.
